CONCATENATE()

What Is CONCATENATE

Concatenate is the formula used to join many things into one

How to use

Click a cell write '=CONCATENATE( value1 , value2 , [value3] ...)' the answer will return as all the parameters joined.

Example

CONCATENATE("I ", "Like ", "Google ", "Sheets ") = I Like Google Sheets.
