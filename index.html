MEDIAN()

What Is MEDIAN

Median is the formula to find the middle of two or more numbers

How to use

Click a cell write '=MEDIAN( value1 , value2 , [value3] ...)' the answer will be the two or one middle number's sum divided by 2.

Example

MEDIAN(850, 851, 852, 853, 854, 855, 856, 857, 858, 859, 860) = 855.5.
